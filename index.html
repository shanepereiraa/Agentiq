FAQ

Questions, answered

How much does it cost and what's included?

Plans start at a one-time setup of ₹14,999 plus ₹4,999/mo (single channel), with our most popular Growth plan at ₹34,999 setup + ₹9,999/mo for all three channels. Every plan includes design, training on your content, launch and ongoing optimisation. GST is extra.

How fast can we go live?

Most bots are live within 7 days. After a quick kickoff call we collect your menu/services, FAQs and policies, build and train the assistant, test it with you, then deploy across your chosen channels. No coding required on your end.

Which channels and tools do you integrate with?

WhatsApp (official Business API), Instagram DMs, and a website chat widget — all powered by one assistant. We also connect to CRMs, Google Sheets, payment links (Razorpay/UPI), Shopify and booking calendars so leads and orders flow straight into your existing tools.

Does it support Hindi and a human takeover?

Yes — your assistant replies fluently in English, Hindi and Hinglish, and we can add regional languages on request. Whenever a customer asks for a person or the query is sensitive, the bot does a smooth handoff to your team and notifies them instantly, so nothing falls through the cracks.

Where is our data stored, and who owns it?

Your data and conversations belong to you. We use encrypted, India-compliant infrastructure, share access only with your authorised team, and never sell or reuse your data to train anyone else's bot. You can export your data or request deletion at any time.
